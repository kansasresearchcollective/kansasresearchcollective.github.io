CURFxKRC Ambassador Program

The CURFxKRC Ambassador Program empowers students to be research advocates by preparing them to assume an ambassadorial role at the CURF and across campus.


Interested in becoming an ambassador this fall? Don't hesitate to reach out.

01

Outreach

Promote CURF and KRC resources in lectures and at campus-wide events like Union Fest and the Undergraduate Research Symposium.

02

Mentorship

Mentor high school students and first-year undergraduate students that are interested in conducting research in your discipline.

03

Office Hours

Host office hours and workshops that prepare students to publish and present their research at the University of Kansas and beyond.

Have a question about an application or project? Stop by our CURFxKRC Ambassador Office Hours.

The CURF Ambassador Program is exclusive to students attending the University of Kansas' Lawrence Campus.
